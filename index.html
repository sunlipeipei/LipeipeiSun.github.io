Layouts and optimization | building exterior

The objective of this project is to analyze and optimize the layout of wall panels and windows on a building facade, incorporating both performance requirements and aesthetic preferences. The project employed discrete mathematics, with a focus on combinatorics and probability, to systematically explore layout options.
